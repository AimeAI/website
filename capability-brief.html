AIME Intelligence — Capability Brief

February 2026

Company Overview

AIME Intelligence is a solo-operator AI consultancy founded and led by Mani Mehramooz. Based in Toronto, Canada, AIME specializes in deploying artificial intelligence solutions for defense, government, and enterprise clients. The firm is veteran-owned, with the principal holding prior Canadian Armed Forces service and an active security clearance. AIME operates with a lean, high-trust model: one operator, no overhead, direct accountability.

Core Capabilities

  • Threat Detection & Intelligence — AI-powered systems for real-time threat identification, pattern analysis, and anomaly detection across structured and unstructured data sources.
  • Process Automation — End-to-end automation of manual workflows using LLMs, agent orchestration, and custom tooling. Focus on reducing human error and accelerating decision cycles.
  • Decision Support Systems — AI-augmented briefing tools, risk scoring models, and operational dashboards that surface actionable intelligence for commanders and executives.
  • Secure AI Architecture — Design and deployment of AI systems that meet security requirements for classified and sensitive environments.
