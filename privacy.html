Privacy Policy

Effective: February 2026

AIME Intelligence ("we," "us") operates aimeintelligence.com. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-focused, cookie-free analytics tool. Plausible does not collect personal data, does not use cookies, and is fully GDPR-compliant. All metrics are aggregated and anonymous. No individual visitor can be identified.

Contact Form

Our contact form is processed by Formspree. When you submit the form, your name, email address, and message are transmitted to Formspree and forwarded to AIME Intelligence. This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list or share your information with third parties.

Cookies & Tracking

This site does not use cookies. We do not deploy behavioral tracking scripts, retargeting pixels, or third-party marketing tags of any kind.

Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or any other purpose. Data submitted through the contact form is shared only with AIME Intelligence for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to complete the conversation or engagement. You may request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.
